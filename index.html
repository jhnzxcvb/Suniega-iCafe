ABOUT US

Suniega iCafe/Suniega Comshop is a family-owned internet cafe that has been proudly serving our community since 2016. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide accessible internet services to our customers. Initially, we started with a traditional server-client setup, offering a reliable environment for work, study, and leisure. As we continued to grow and evolve, we experimented with different business models, including the popular piso net setup. However, after careful consideration and feedback from our valued patrons, we have transitioned back to our roots with a renewed focus on the server-client setup. Today, Suniega iCafe offers a comfortable and conducive space for individuals and families to connect, work, and play. In addition to our internet services, we provide printing facilities, online assistance, and various other amenities to enhance your experience with us. We are dedicated to maintaining our commitment to quality service, reliability, and customer satisfaction as we embark on this new chapter. Thank you for your continued support, and we look forward to welcoming you to Suniega iCafe.
